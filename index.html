About Me

I am a Biotechnology Engineering graduate with research experience in Bioinformatics from Siddaganga Institute of Technology, an autonomous institute, affliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University. I am passionate about exploring the fascinating realms of Structural Biophysics, Systems Biology, Computational Biology, and Bioinformatics. These interdisciplinary fields offer a unique opportunity to decipher the intricate mechanisms governing biological systems at various levels, from atomic structures to complex networks. I am eager to engage in cutting-edge research within these domains, as they hold the potential to uncover crucial insights into the fundamental processes of life and pave the way for groundbreaking discoveries in the world of biology.

About Me

I hold a degree in Biotechnology Engineering from Siddaganga Institute of Technology, an autonomous institution affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University, and possess valuable research experience in Bioinformatics gained through my collaborative ventures with several institutes. My fervor lies in delving into the captivating domains of Structural Biophysics, Systems Biology, Computational Biology, and Bioinformatics. I firmly believe that these interdisciplinary fields provide a unique opportunity to unravel the intricate mechanisms governing biological systems, spanning from atomic-level structures to complex networks. I am eagerly poised to immerse myself in cutting-edge research within these realms, as they possess the potential to unveil crucial insights into the fundamental processes of life, paving the way for groundbreaking discoveries in the realm of biology.
